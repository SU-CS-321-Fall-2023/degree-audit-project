Self Skills are individual skills that vary between characters. They can only be learned by reaching a certain level.

Special Skills were introduced in Samurai Warriors 2: Xtreme Legends. Similar to self skills, they provide characters with unique properties once acquired.

Regular Ability Skills augment a character's own stats.

Regular Growth Skills accelerate a character's stat growth upon leveling up.

Regular Battle Skills enhance different aspects of a character's fighting abilities.

Regular Special Skills provide a plethora of beneficial effects to the character.

Table Key

    Character

  • A playable character that you can acquire skills and weapons for.

    • Moveset Type

  • There are three Moveset Types in Samurai Warriors 2.

    1. Charge

  • This is a style that relies on Y-button attacks, with 3 inputs total for each charge attack finisher with 8 total inputs for the normal attack chain. Charge attacks C1~C4 have three general extensions each.
  • This is the moveset type closest to the first title of the Samurai Warriors game series.

    • 2. Normal

  • X-button intensive moveset up to 12 inputs for the normal attack chain, and is given single-input charge attacks from C1 to C8 (there are exceptions for some characters).
  • Normal attack chains are stressed up to 12 inputs total, and each charge attack property follows a scheme that's often much closer to the singular-input style of the Dynasty Warriors series (from C1-to-C6). A majority of C7 attacks for this moveset type tends to be an unblockable grab.

    • 3. Special

  • A mix of both styles; moveset is structurally close to the Charge type, but the character has a balanced reliance on X-button and Y-button attacks (8 inputs total for the former chain, 2 inputs total for the latter). C1~C4 have two inputs each.
  • RB-button Special Skills of this type have three levels per extra input with varying effects.

    • Self Skill

  • Individual skills that vary between characters. Can only be learned by reaching a certain level. (More info coming soon...)

    • Weapon Type

  • The specific weapon that the character uses in combat. Weapons have multiple tiers. Weapons can be acquired from crates on the battlefield and from defeating enemy officers. (More info coming soon...)
